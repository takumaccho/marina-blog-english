Pushed Forward by Everyone's Voices

The response to my last post about possibly starting a fan club has been overwhelming. I've received so many messages - "Fan club sounds great!" and "I'll definitely join!" - and I'm honestly moved to tears.

Your Voices Gave Me Courage

After reading through all your heartfelt messages, I realized something profound: your words have become my greatest source of strength to move forward.

Each comment, each message of encouragement, each expression of support - they all added up to give me the courage I needed to make a decision I couldn't have made alone.

I've Made My Decision

After carefully considering all your requests and feedback, I've decided to launch a fan club.

But even as I say this, I still have lingering anxiety. Do I really deserve to create such a space? Will people actually join? These doubts haven't completely disappeared.

However, your voices have pushed me forward. You've shown me that there's genuine interest and support, and that gives me the courage to try.

Starting with a Photo Collection

Rather than immediately launching the full fan club, I want to start with something many of you have requested: a photo book.

This has been one of the most frequently asked-for items, and I think it's a perfect first project. It will give me a chance to see if there's real interest, and whether I can successfully create something that you'll love.

In a way, this photo collection will be a test run. It will help determine the viability of future fan club activities and give me confidence moving forward.

I Couldn't Have Done This Alone

I need to be honest: I couldn't have made this decision by myself. The anxiety would have been too much. The self-doubt would have won.

But your consistent encouragement, your enthusiasm, your belief in me - all of that gave me the strength to take this new step.

This isn't just my project. It's our journey together. You're not just passive supporters; you're active participants in this growth process.

What's Next

I'm going to start working on the photo collection. I want to create something beautiful, something meaningful, something that captures this moment in our shared journey.

There's still a lot to figure out - logistics, content, distribution. But I'm excited to tackle these challenges with your support behind me.

Thank You

Thank you for pushing me forward. Thank you for believing in me even when I struggle to believe in myself. Thank you for being part of this journey.

Every step I take is possible because of you. Every milestone I reach is a shared achievement. I'm so grateful to have you with me.

Let's continue this journey together!

If you have any thoughts, suggestions, or just want to share your excitement, please reach out via LINE. Your input matters, and I want to hear from you as we move forward with these plans.
