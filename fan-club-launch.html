We're Opening a Fan Club!

It's finally happening. Tomorrow, I'm opening a fan club.

I'm excited, nervous, and filled with so many emotions I can barely process them all. This feels like a huge step forward - one that I couldn't have imagined taking just months ago.

Connecting More Deeply

The primary reason I'm creating this fan club is simple: I want to connect more with everyone who has supported me on this journey.

You've been with me through my struggles, my fears, my small victories, and my moments of growth. You've encouraged me when I felt lost and celebrated with me when I achieved something I thought was impossible.

A fan club feels like the natural next step - a space where we can deepen our connection and share experiences more intimately.

Still Finding My Way

I have to be honest: I'm still not entirely sure if this path suits me. There's still uncertainty, still questions about whether I'm capable of creating something truly valuable for you.

But I've learned that growth rarely comes with certainty. Sometimes you have to take the leap even when you're not completely sure where you'll land.

A Special Commemorative Gift

To celebrate the opening of the fan club, I want to offer you something special: a photo collection featuring swimsuit photos from my beach visit.

These are completely unpublished images - photos I haven't shared on social media. They represent a significant moment in my journey, and I want to share them with those who have been most supportive.

I'll be offering this collection for only three days. I have to admit, I have mixed feelings about this level of exposure, and the limited timeframe helps me feel more comfortable with sharing something so personal.

I Need Your Feedback

As I embark on this fan club journey, I want to hear your honest impressions. What works? What doesn't? What would you like to see?

I'm still exploring what's best, and I want to develop the fan club thoughtfully based on your responses. Your feedback will help shape what this community becomes.

This isn't just my project - it's ours. I want to build something that truly serves and celebrates our connection.

Looking Forward

I don't know exactly what the future holds. I don't know if I'll be good at running a fan club. I don't know if I can create content that consistently meets your expectations.

But I know that I want to try. I know that your support has brought me this far. And I know that whatever comes next, we'll face it together.

Thank You

Thank you for making this possible. Thank you for believing in me. Thank you for pushing me forward when I was too afraid to move on my own.

This fan club exists because of you. And I'm so grateful to be taking this next step with you by my side.

Stay tuned for the official launch!

Check LINE for the gift announcement and details about the fan club. If you haven't subscribed yet, now is the perfect time. I can't wait to connect with you in this new space.
