Going to the Beach After 10 Years

I did it. I actually did it. After 10 years, I returned to the ocean.

The Journey There

As I made my way to the beach, I felt a mix of emotions - anxiety, uncertainty, anticipation, and fear all swirling together. My heart was racing, and I kept questioning whether I was really ready for this.

But I had committed to this challenge. I had told you all I would do it. And with your support behind me, I kept moving forward.

The Moment of Arrival

When I finally arrived and saw the ocean stretching out before me, I was overwhelmed with emotion.

"Wow... it's been 10 years since I've been to the sea!"

The reality of that moment hit me hard. A decade. Ten whole years I had stayed away from something I used to love, all because of fear and insecurity.

Because of Your Support

As I stood there, taking it all in, I realized something profound: I was only able to return to this place because of everyone's support.

Your encouragement, your kind words, your belief in me - all of it gave me the strength to face this fear. I couldn't have done this alone.

The Response

When I shared my beach photos and swimming attire with you all, the response was overwhelming. Comments like "the swimsuit looks perfect on you!" and messages about how my smile gave others energy - reading them brought tears to my eyes.

I never imagined that my small act of courage could have such a positive impact on others.

The Best Summer Memory

This experience has created what I can only describe as the best summer memory I've had in years.

It wasn't just about going to the beach. It was about reclaiming a part of myself I thought I had lost. It was about proving to myself that growth is possible, that change is possible, that I am capable of more than I believed.

Looking Forward

I'm so grateful for this journey and for all of you who have been part of it. This is just one milestone, but it represents so much more - a turning point, a moment of transformation.

Thank you for walking this path with me. Thank you for believing in me when I struggled to believe in myself.

Let's keep growing together!

If you've read this far, I'd love to hear from you. Feel free to reach out via LINE - knowing that my story resonates with you means everything to me.
