Endless Notifications

Hello everyone, it's Risa!

Thank you so much for watching my third project 🫶

Today, I want to share with you some of the feedback I received!

So Happy to Receive So Many Responses

This project has been viewed by so many people, and the notifications about incoming feedback messages didn't stop ringing for quite a while 😳

I carefully read each and every one of them.

The most common responses I received from everyone were:

"I watch it every day and it heals me" and "Thanks to you, I'm getting energy today too!"

Every time I read comments like these, I'm the one receiving so much energy, and it fills me with warmth ☺️

Words of Kindness

Just as frequently, I receive messages like:

"Please don't push yourself too hard" and "Go at your own pace"

These are such gentle, caring words from people who are worried about me.

When I read them, I feel "So many people care about me this much," and it truly makes me feel blessed 🫶

The Truth Is...

While receiving all these kind and caring words, I have to confess something: after finishing the last photoshoot, I actually got a bit sick 💦

I think it's partly because of the changing seasons and the sudden cold weather 😭

But looking back at everything up to this point, I feel like I've been running at full speed until I released this project. I didn't think I was pushing myself too hard, but maybe my body was telling me "Hey, let's take a little break."

Taking Time to Recover

So I'm going to properly take care of my health and rest up before moving forward to the next step.

(I'll still do my best to keep up with my regular social media posts, so I hope you'll continue watching!)

Learning to Listen

This experience has taught me something important about listening to my body. I've always wanted to keep going, to keep giving you all more, to keep growing without pause.

But maybe growth also means knowing when to rest. Maybe taking care of myself is part of being able to continue this journey with all of you.

Your messages telling me not to push too hard - they've taken on new meaning now. You were seeing something I wasn't ready to see yet.

Gratitude

I'm so grateful for every single message. The ones that gave me energy, the ones that made me smile, and especially the ones that reminded me to take care of myself.

You've created such a supportive space where I can be honest about both my victories and my vulnerabilities. That's incredibly precious to me.

Moving Forward Mindfully

I promise to take this recovery time seriously and to listen to what my body and mind need. When I come back, I want to come back stronger and healthier.

This isn't goodbye or a long break - just a pause to breathe and recharge. Your support makes even this possible.

For those of you who read all the way to the end... I'll share something special with you:

I'm sharing my activity LINE! 💭

I respond occasionally when the mood strikes!

If you'd like, please feel free to reach out.

If you message me saying "I read your blog," I'd be so happy! 💕
