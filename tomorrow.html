It's Finally Tomorrow

Tomorrow is the day I've decided to publicly share bikini photos. I'm feeling incredibly anxious about this step.

Looking Back at My Past

Middle school and high school were really difficult times for me. I was deeply troubled by my body image, and those feelings still linger in the back of my mind.

Even now, I find myself wondering: "Will people accept me?" I worry about potential ridicule and negative comments.

The Support That Changed My Perspective

But something has been gradually changing within me. The warm comments and support I've received on social media have made me feel like maybe, just maybe, I might be okay as I am.

When followers asked about my content preferences and showed genuine interest, it meant so much to me. Your kind words have given me the courage to take this step.

Overcoming My Fears

Despite my ongoing nervousness, I feel motivated to overcome these past limitations. This feels like an opportunity for self-acceptance and personal transformation.

I'm not saying I'm completely confident - far from it. But I'm ready to try. I'm ready to face my fears and see what happens on the other side.

A Request to My Readers

If you do view my content tomorrow, I have one request: please watch with kind hearts. Your compassionate support means everything to me as I take this vulnerable step.

I know this might seem like a small thing to some, but for me, it represents years of struggle and growth. It's about more than just photos - it's about accepting myself and breaking free from the insecurities that have held me back.

Thank you for being part of this journey with me.

If you've read this far, feel free to reach out to me via LINE. I try to respond when I can, and I love hearing from readers who connect with my story.
