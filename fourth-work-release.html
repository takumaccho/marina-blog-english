Releasing My 4th Work Tomorrow!

Hello everyone, it's Risa.

Today, I want to announce my Halloween project to everyone!

I Shot It!

Remember the Halloween project I mentioned in my last blog post? Well, I completed the photoshoot recently!

I wore three cute cosplay outfits, and I even rented a stylish photoshoot studio, so I was feeling really excited ☺️

By the way, about which cosplays I did...

① The cosplay I showed in my last blog post (the fluffy one that looks like a cat? A wolf? That one!)
② Maid outfit
③ Apron look

These are the three 🫶

As for the maid and apron outfits, the costumes were quite daring... 😳

On the day of the shoot, my excitement and energy were so high that as I kept filming, I thought "I want to show everyone even more," and I got more and more bold *laughs*

It Became an Over 1-Hour Work

Because of that, this project, just like the last one, shows "my true self, as I am."

However, since I tried cosplay this time, it became quite a different work from the last one ☺️

I hope you'll get excited watching me gradually go from cosplay to "full nudity" 🫶

And the total recording time for this work is over 1 hour! ✨

During filming, I really got into it and ended up shooting a lot *laughs*

I feel like I was more relaxed than last time, and I think my expressions and atmosphere are more natural!

The Journey Through Three Costumes

Each costume represents a different aspect of playfulness and vulnerability. Starting with the fluffy cat/wolf costume - cute and fun, then transitioning to the classic maid look with its own charm, and finally the apron ensemble.

What made this shoot special was how my confidence grew throughout the process. Each costume change felt like shedding a layer of hesitation, becoming more comfortable with being seen.

More Relaxed, More Natural

One thing I'm really proud of with this project is how much more relaxed I felt compared to my previous shoots. When you're more comfortable, it shows in your expressions, your body language, your energy.

I wasn't just performing - I was genuinely enjoying myself. And I think you'll be able to feel that joy when you watch it.

Over an Hour of Content

I honestly didn't plan for it to be this long! But once I got started, the creative energy just flowed. I wanted to capture every angle, every transition, every moment of the journey from cosplay to my natural self.

This is my longest project yet, and I poured so much into it. I hope the length doesn't feel excessive - instead, I hope it feels like a complete experience, a full story.

Releasing Tomorrow!

I'm releasing this Halloween work tomorrow! ✨

I hope you're looking forward to it ☺️

Since it's Halloween...

If you don't watch, I'll have to play a trick on you~! (Just kidding, of course *laughs* ☺️)

A Different Kind of Vulnerability

This project feels different from my previous work. Yes, it's still about showing my authentic self, but the journey through costumes adds a layer of storytelling that wasn't there before.

It's about transformation, about shedding not just clothes but also inhibitions, about discovering confidence through play and creativity.

Gratitude

Creating this made me realize how far I've come. From someone who was afraid to be seen to someone who can express themselves this freely - that journey has been possible because of your support.

Thank you for making it safe for me to be this vulnerable, this playful, this authentically myself.

Thank you for reading to the end.

I'll be sending the announcement about this work through LINE tomorrow.
