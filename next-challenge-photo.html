About My Next Challenge

The fan club launch went far better than I could have imagined. Now I'm ready to share what's next.

Beyond My Expectations

I was so anxious about launching the fan club. Would anyone actually join? Would people be disappointed? Would I be able to create something worthwhile?

But the response has been incredible. Far more people registered than I anticipated, and the feedback has been overwhelmingly positive. I'm genuinely moved by your support.

Your Courage Inspired Others

When I shared the photo collection gift, something unexpected happened. Several people told me that seeing my courage to share those photos inspired them to face their own struggles.

I never imagined that my vulnerability could have such an impact. Hearing that I've helped others find their own courage means more to me than I can express.

A New Creative Drive

The fan club experience has sparked something in me - a desire to pursue more varied expressions and creative works in this special space we're building together.

I still have fears about how my core audience might react to bolder creative choices. But I've found confidence in knowing that you accept my authentic self. That acceptance emboldens me to gradually take creative risks.

The Next Challenge: A Bolder Collection

I'm announcing my next project: a second photo collection that will be bolder than the first.

The photoshoot is scheduled for tomorrow, and I'm both nervous and excited. This collection will push me out of my comfort zone in ways I haven't experienced before.

But my desire to bring joy to you - to create something special for those who support me - outweighs my anxiety. I'm ready to take this leap.

Release Information

The second photo collection will debut on October 6th and will be available for a limited three-day period.

This limited release helps me feel more comfortable sharing something so bold. Knowing it's temporary makes the vulnerability feel more manageable.

Creative Expression in a Safe Space

What makes this possible is the space we've created together. The fan club isn't just about content - it's about having a safe environment where I can express myself creatively without fear of harsh judgment.

You've given me that gift. Your acceptance and encouragement have created a foundation where I can explore new forms of self-expression.

Moving Forward Together

This is just the beginning. I have so many ideas for creative projects, new ways to connect, new challenges to take on.

And I want to do it all with you by my side. Your support makes everything possible. Your enthusiasm gives me strength. Your acceptance allows me to be brave.

Thank You

Thank you for making the fan club launch such a success. Thank you for your encouragement. Thank you for being part of this creative journey.

I can't wait to share this next collection with you. Let's continue growing together.

Don't miss the release!

Sign up for LINE notifications to receive updates about the October 6th release. This limited three-day collection is something I'm incredibly proud to share with you.
