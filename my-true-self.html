Of course, my anxiety hasn't completely disappeared.

I'd be so happy if you could watch it and share your thoughts with me!

This Was a Big Step

This project represents something significant for me. It's not just about the content itself, but about what it symbolizes in my journey.

When I started sharing my story, I never imagined I'd reach this point. Each step has built on the previous one, and your support has made every single step possible.

There were moments during the shoot when I felt nervous, moments when I questioned myself, but there were also moments when I felt incredibly free and authentic.

The Filming Experience

Filming this video was different from taking photos. Speaking to the camera, being in motion, staying present for an extended period - it all required a different kind of vulnerability.

But it also allowed me to express myself in ways that photos couldn't capture. I could share my thoughts directly, talk about my feelings in real-time, and let you see more of who I really am.

The 30+ minutes ended up including so much - moments of reflection, moments of nervousness, and moments where I just tried to be completely myself.

Looking Forward to Your Response
